

read
    reads sensor values
long d/duration:<duration> s/samples:<samples> p/period/t/interval:<interval>
    d/duration
        total duration of measurement
        <duration>
            integer (seconds), possibly prefixed with "s" (seconds) or "m" for minutes
    s/samples:<samples>
        total number of samples
    p/period/t/interval:<interval>
        measurement interval in seconds, possibly prefixed with "s" (seconds) or "m" for minutes

    exactly 2 of these options must be specified
